Notes

— In the 2011-2012 National Survey of Children’s Health, respondents were asked about whether their children’s vision had been tested with pictures, shapes or letters. — Screening rates for black children in Montana, North Dakota and Wyoming are not shown because the sample sizes were too small.

Source: National Survey of Children’s Health (2011-2012); National Center for Children’s Vision and Eye Health at Prevent Blindness: “Children’s Vision and Eye Health: A Snapshot of Current National Issues” (2016)
