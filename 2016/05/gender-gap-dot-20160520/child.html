Women Used To Vote More Republican Than Men. No Longer.

Women supported Republican Dwight Eisenhower even more than men did in both of his elections (1952 and 1956). Since 1992, women have reliably voted more Democratic than men. Most recently, in 2012, Democrat Barack Obama’s margin of victory among women was 11 percentage points, while men favored Republican Mitt Romney by 7 percentage points.

Notes

— Data for 2016 are from the May 23, 2016, NBC/WSJ poll.
— Data through 1972 are based on Gallup’s final pre-election polls.

Source: Gallup (1952 through 1972), Roper Center (1976 through 2012), NBC/WSJ poll (released May 23, 2016).
