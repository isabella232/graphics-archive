Trump’s Recent Travels Have Not Been To Competitive States

Donald Trump became the apparent Republican nominee after he won the Indiana primary and his two main competitors dropped out. Since then, most of his public appearances have been in states with upcoming primaries — but not states of competitive value to Republicans in the general election. Below, where Trump has gone and how NPR has rated that state.
