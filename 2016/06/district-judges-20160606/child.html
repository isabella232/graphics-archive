Whites and Men Are Disproportionately Represented Among District Court Judges

Active U.S. District Court judges don’t quite look like the U.S. population as a whole. They are more likely to be white non-Hispanics and far more likely to be men than the rest of the nation.

Notes

— “White” here means “white non-Hispanic.”

Source: U.S. Census BureauCongressional Research Service
