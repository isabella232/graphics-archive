GOP Ads Aired In South Carolina

Jeb Bush and his affiliated super PAC, Right to Rise USA, have aired more ads overall in South Carolina, but Ted Cruz and Marco Rubio (and their affiliated super PACs) have aired more in the lead-up to the South Carolina Republican primary on Feb. 20.

Notes

— Figures are from January 1, 2015, to February 14, 2016, and include the Greenville, Charleston, Columbia, Myrtle Beach, Charlotte, Savannah and Augusta media markets. Numbers include broadcast television.
— Figures for Jeb Bush include ads funded by Right to Rise USA. Figures for Marco Rubio include ads funded by Conservative Solutions PAC and Conservative Solutions Project. Figures for Ted Cruz include ads funded by Keep the Promise I and Stand for Truth.

Source: Kantar Media/CMAG with analysis by the Wesleyan Media Project
