High GOP Turnout In All Four Tuesday States

Republicans continued to post big bumps in turnout numbers. Democrats had a rare turnout bump in Michigan (and that’s itself because the state didn’t hold a real primary in 2008).

Notes

Could not obtain data for Hawaii 2008. In Idaho, Republicans held primaries in 2008 and 2016 but a caucus in 2012. Michigan’s Democratic primary in 2008 may have been depressed: The Democratic party penalized the state for scheduling its primary too early, and Barack Obama and John Edwards withdrew from the contest.

Source: U.S. Elections Project, AP via NPR
