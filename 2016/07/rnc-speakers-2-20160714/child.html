Who’s Speaking At The Republican Convention?

The speakers list for the 2016 RNC convention include politicians, businesspeople, and five Trumps, among others.

Notes

— “Governors” and “Congress” include former governors and former members. “Other Politicians” includes former politicians who do not fit into those categories (Michael Mukasey, etc.) and state-level officials.

— “Military” includes Karen Vaughn, known for being the mother of a fallen military member.

— “Party Figures” includes Alex Smith, chair of the College RNC.

— “Family” does not include Donald Trump himself.

Source: Republican National Committee, New York Times
