Notes

— Underemployment: Workers who are unemployed and looking for work; workers who have stopped looking for work; and workers who are working part time for economic reasons but would like to work full time. (It’s also referred to as the U-6 rate.)
— Hourly wages: Earnings of production and nonsupervisory employees in private nonfarm jobs
— Unemployment figures are seasonally adjusted.

Source: Bureau of Labor Statistics via St. Louis Fed (unemployment, underemployment, hourly wages)
