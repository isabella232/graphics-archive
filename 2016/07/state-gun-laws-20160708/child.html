How Five State Legislatures Responded To Recent Mass Shootings

We looked at five states that had a high-profile mass shooting in 2015 — California, Colorado, Oregon, South Carolina and Tennessee — and gun-related legislation that has been proposed since the shooting. The results are consistent with what the longer-term Harvard study showed: States with Republican legislatures tended to pass laws that eased gun restrictions, while states with Democratic legislatures tended to pass laws that strengthened gun restrictions (or nothing at all).
