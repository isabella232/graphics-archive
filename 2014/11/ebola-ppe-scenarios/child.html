You're Inside An Ebola Treatment Center, Wearing Personal Protective Equipment…

This happens: What do you do?
Your nose itches. Restrain from touching.
Vomit spatters on your apron. Wash off with 0.5% chlorine. (Bucket with solution should be present in every room.)
You have to cough or sneeze. Do not touch your mouth, mask or shield. Just cough or sneeze through the mask.
You feel very very hot. Notify the person you entered with and get out together.
You cannot find your buddy. Call out his or her name.
There is a fly or insect in the goggles. Do not remove the goggles. Notify the person you entered with and get out together.
You need to urinate. Notify the person you entered with and get out together.
Your goggles fog and you cannot see. Notify the person you entered with and get out together.
You feel faint. Notify the person you entered with and get out together.

Source: World Health Organization ebola clinical training facilitator guide (Sept. 15, 2014)
