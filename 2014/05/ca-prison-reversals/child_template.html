Percentage Of Parole Decisions Reversed By Governor

Govs. Gray Davis (1999-2003) and Arnold Schwarzenegger (2003-2011) reversed a majority of the parole recommendations for murderers that came across their desks. Gov. Jerry Brown, who took office in 2011, has reversed less than 20 percent.
