New Orleans Schools, By Type

Nine years after Katrina, an education system has been transformed. The Orleans Parish School Board directly runs just six remaining schools; the rest of the taxpayer-funded schools, including the entire Recovery School District, are charter-managed. New Orleans also has the highest percentage of students attending private and parochial schools of any large city, according to a recent study.

Notes

“Traditional” refers to public schools directly run by local school districts. “Other” is the New Orleans Center for Creative Arts, an academy run separately through the state. Private school figures are not available for 2014-15; totals for 2013-14 are shown instead.
