Half The World’s Teens Live in These 7 Countries

A third of all 10- to 19-year-olds live in India and China. Throughout this story we’ll highlight these two countries in orange.
The remaining five countries with the highest number of 10- to 19-year-olds — Indonesia, Nigeria, the United States, Pakistan and Brazil — bring the total population represented up to just over 50 percent of the world’s total.

Source: World Bank Population Estimates for 2015
