New Orleans Has Third-Highest Rate Of ‘Opportunity Youth’

In the New Orleans metropolitan statistical area, an estimated 18.2 percent of 16- to 24-year-olds are neither working nor in school.

Notes: Lighter bars indicate margin of error. Analysis based on 2013 American Community Survey Public Use Microdata Sample data.
