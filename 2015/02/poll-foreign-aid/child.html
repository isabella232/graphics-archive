Public Overestimates Share Of Budget Going To Foreign Aid

In a Kaiser Family Foundation Poll in December, respondents guessed, on average, that the federal government spends 26% of its budget on foreign aid. The actual share? 1 percent or less.

“Just your best guess, what percentage of the federal budget is spent on foreign aid?”

Source: Kaiser Family Foundation Health Tracking Poll conducted Dec. 2-9, 2014
