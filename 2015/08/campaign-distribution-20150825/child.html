Donations To 2016 Presidential Candidates, By Size

Percent of candidates’ committee and superPAC donations from different donor groups. Charts are ordered by amount of money raised (largest to smallest — that is, Jeb Bush to Lincoln Chafee).

Notes

— Scott Walker and Chris Christie’s figures only represent superPAC donations, as they did not announce their candidacies in time to report data on their campaigns’ donors.
— Jim Webb and John Kasich are not represented because they and/or their associated superPACs have not yet had to file donation data with the FEC.

Source: Campaign Finance Institute
